Goldendoodle

Ebba

November 11, 2017

Ebba was born on July 18, 2016. She is a F1B Goldendoodle. The F1B tag means she is 75% poodle, 25% goldendoodle.

Ebba has the sweetest personality out of any dog I have ever met (I know everyone says that about their dog). She absolutely loves everyone including humans, dogs, and cats. Our cat Winston is not her biggest fan though.

She occassionally accompanies me to work at a nursing home and visits with the residents.

“She had 4 weeks of training prior to coming home with us, and we are constantly trying to keep up with her commands and response."
